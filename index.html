what is C4M0?

C4M0 is a group of people who are interested in sniper rifles and D.M.R`s (designated marksman rifles). This is an open group so everyone can join this group. The founder of this group is Cas Minke. This group has been established to bring people with the same interests together, especially in the game world. the moto of this group is “Accuracy is Key”.
